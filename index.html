The Person Behind the Projects

I’ve always been the type of person who can’t just look at a device without wondering what’s happening underneath the surface. That curiosity led me to Electrical & Electronics Engineering. There’s something satisfying about taking a complex problem—whether it's a messy circuit diagram or a stubborn piece of code—and finding that one elegant solution that makes everything click.

Right now, my world is a mix of high-precision hardware design and the fast-paced world of software. I don't see them as separate; to me, building a noise-free amplifier requires the same focus as crafting a seamless user interface.
